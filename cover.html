Martin and his partner, The Hat, collect overdue fines and books for a New Orleans Occult Library.

While in Chicago to collect books from a guy who's skipped town, they become pawns inside a power struggle with a wanna-be ganster, a Cannibal who's Martin's old boss, and the vengeful daughter of an ancient French family who's homecoming plants the seed of Hell's burdgeoning Civil War in The Dark Easy.
